# World Context: Stellar Chronicles Book 2

This document captures all known people, places, organizations, objects, events, rules, and relationships established in the provided pages of Stellar Chronicles Book 2. It is intended to be comprehensive and precise for consistent future writing.

## Story Overview
- Genre and sub-genre
  - Post-apocalyptic speculative fiction with superpowered individuals (Lunars/Solars/Stellars), espionage, dystopian tyranny, and resistance.
- Narrative tone
  - Tense, high-stakes, gritty with moments of warmth and humor; largely dark and suspenseful.
- Central premise and core conflict
  - After crippling Abe’s Los Angeles operation, Freedom Fields faces a flood of refugees and new threats while Abe resurges in Denmark with elite “Top-Level” Lunars and a renewed plan to augment his power. Isaac, a rare Solar, struggles to control his abilities while a reunited Team One/LARS network seeks to gather intel and counter Abe’s growing empire. 
- Major themes
  - Freedom vs. control; the ethics and limits of power; sacrifice; the costs of leadership; memory and trauma; bonds of family (biological and adopted); trust vs. manipulation (dreams, trances).
- Point of view / narrative perspective
  - Third-person close, largely following Winston, Isaac, Lawrence, Samuel; scenes sometimes shift to other characters (Rose/Martin/Jens/etc.).
- Time span covered
  - Roughly six months after the LA operation (Book 1). The current action unfolds over days-to-weeks: Winston’s Denmark infiltration and return; Denmark mission prep/launch; Isaac’s Great Falls/Baldy arc; Bozeman trap; Denmark infiltration by LARS and prison break; Samuel’s discovery and fatal confrontation.

- Full name: Abraham (“Abe”) — titles: Tyrant; ruler centered in Denmark; formerly controlled LA.
